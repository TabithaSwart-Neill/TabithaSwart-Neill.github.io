I am an independent disability support worker to the level of social-, admin- and domestic support. My passion is to support people to pursue and accomplish their goals, and I contribute in creative ways, which in itself, keeps my days interesting and I go the extra mile for my clients, just because I CARE. Over the past twenty years, my main focus was to help individuals capture and digitise their life stories. This would include memories, souvenirs, accomplishments and lots more, all preserved into digital formats and then combined into a production that could be showcased and enjoyed with friends and family on special occasions. I am currently part of the She Codes Plus program in Brisbane to develop and enhance my tech skills.
